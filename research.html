My research interests are homological algebra, homotopical algebra, and noncommutative geometry.

Specifically, I am interested in various constructions and properties of Hochschild and cyclic homology, and connect them to other areas, such as noncommutative geometry, (derived) algebraic geometry, and string topology.

I am also interested in the applications of algebra to artificial intelligence (AI).
